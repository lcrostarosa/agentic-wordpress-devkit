# Local SEO Checklist for Service Businesses

## Google Business Profile (Critical)

- [ ] Profile claimed and verified
- [ ] Business name matches real-world name exactly
- [ ] Primary category is accurate and specific (e.g., "Home Automation Company" not "Technology Company")
- [ ] Secondary categories added for all relevant services
- [ ] Service area defined (radius or specific cities/zip codes)
- [ ] Business hours current and accurate
- [ ] Phone number matches website
- [ ] Website URL is correct
- [ ] Business description filled out (750 characters, include keywords naturally)
- [ ] At least 10 photos uploaded (storefront, team, completed projects)
- [ ] Services listed with descriptions
- [ ] Posts published in the last 30 days
- [ ] Q&A section has owner-provided answers
- [ ] Review count >= 10 (minimum viable for trust)
- [ ] Review rating >= 4.0
- [ ] Owner responds to all reviews (positive and negative)

## On-Page Local Signals

- [ ] City/region name appears in title tag of homepage
- [ ] City/region name appears in H1 of homepage
- [ ] City/region name appears in meta description
- [ ] Physical address on website (even if home-based, use a service area statement)
- [ ] Phone number in header (clickable on mobile)
- [ ] Embedded Google Map on contact page
- [ ] NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent across all pages
- [ ] Service area pages exist for each city/neighborhood served (if serving multiple)
- [ ] Individual service pages exist (not one giant services page)

## Structured Data (Schema Markup)

- [ ] LocalBusiness or HomeAndConstructionBusiness schema on homepage
- [ ] Schema includes: name, address, phone, URL, geo coordinates, opening hours
- [ ] Service schema on service pages
- [ ] Review/AggregateRating schema (if reviews displayed on site)
- [ ] BreadcrumbList schema for site navigation
- [ ] FAQ schema on FAQ or service pages (if applicable)

## Technical Foundations

- [ ] Site loads in under 3 seconds on mobile
- [ ] HTTPS enabled (no mixed content warnings)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (passes Google Mobile-Friendly Test)
- [ ] XML sitemap exists and is submitted to Google Search Console
- [ ] robots.txt exists and does not block important pages
- [ ] No duplicate content issues (www vs non-www, http vs https)
- [ ] 404 page exists and is helpful
- [ ] No broken internal links
- [ ] Images are compressed and have alt text
- [ ] Site has a favicon

## Content Signals

- [ ] Unique content on every page (no boilerplate across service pages)
- [ ] Content length >= 300 words on key pages
- [ ] Project gallery or portfolio with descriptions
- [ ] Testimonials with client names and locations
- [ ] Certifications and partnerships displayed (e.g., Control4 Certified Dealer)
- [ ] About page with team photos and bios
- [ ] Blog or resource section with at least 5 posts (ideally 10+)

## Citation & Directory Presence

Priority directories for home services:

- [ ] Google Business Profile
- [ ] Yelp
- [ ] BBB (Better Business Bureau)
- [ ] Houzz
- [ ] HomeAdvisor / Angi
- [ ] Manufacturer dealer locator (Control4, Savant, Crestron, Lutron)
- [ ] Local chamber of commerce
- [ ] Facebook business page
- [ ] LinkedIn company page
- [ ] Nextdoor business page
- [ ] Apple Maps (via Apple Business Connect)
- [ ] Bing Places for Business

NAP must be identical across ALL directories.

## Review Strategy

- [ ] Active review request process exists (email after project completion)
- [ ] Review links are easy to find (direct link to Google review form)
- [ ] Reviews mention specific services and location naturally
- [ ] Owner responds to every review within 48 hours
- [ ] Reviews appear on the website (with schema markup)
- [ ] Multiple review platforms have presence (not just Google)

## Competitor Benchmarking Dimensions

When comparing against competitors, check:

1. Google review count and rating
2. Number of indexed pages (site:competitor.com)
3. Domain authority (if Ahrefs available)
4. Service page depth (individual pages vs single page)
5. Blog/content presence and recency
6. Social media activity
7. Structured data implementation
8. Page speed scores
9. Backlink profile (if Ahrefs available)
10. Local pack presence for key queries
